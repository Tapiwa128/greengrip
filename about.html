About Greengrip Energy

Who We Are

Founded in 2010, Greengrip Energy has grown from a small Victoria Falls-based operation to one of Zimbabwe's most trusted providers of sustainable energy and water solutions. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make clean energy and water accessible to all Zimbabweans.

Today, we serve residential, commercial, and agricultural clients across the country, with completed projects in all 10 provinces. Our team of certified technicians and engineers brings together decades of combined experience in renewable energy and water infrastructure.

Thomas Banda
Founder & CEO
